My Wallet

Last year, I saw my friend Ryan post about “the extreme value proposition that exists in leveraging credit card sign-up bonuses to travel the world virtually for free.” After seeing this, I began to follow The Points Guy and Scott's Cheap Flights ; thanks to their expertise, I have amassed enough points to travel to my heart's desire in 2018!

Along the way, I gained a reputation among friends for being the credit card guy. As such, I wanted to make a page to share exactly what cards are in my wallet, what bonuses I have earned, and what cards I recommend for fellow millennials! This page can be found here .
