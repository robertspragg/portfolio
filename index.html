My Wallet

Last year, I saw my friend Ryan post about “the extreme value proposition that exists in leveraging credit card sign-up bonuses to travel the world virtually for free.” After seeing this, I began to follow The Points Guy and others; thanks to their expertise, I have amassed enough Points to travel to my hearts desire in 2018! Along the way, I gained a reputation among friends for being the “credit card guy”. As such, I wanted to make a website to share exactly what cards are in my wallet, what bonuses I have earned, and what cards I recommend for fellow millennials! The site, Millennial Card Management, can be found here .
